Your calorie entries are currently stored only on this device. If you lose access to this device you will lose your data.
Click Login to Dropbox to sync your calorie entries to your Dropbox. Log in anywhere with the same Dropbox account and your entries will appear.

  • This app will not read any personal data from your Dropbox account
  • This app will only have access to it's own folder (/Apps/app-nehmen) and not your entire Dropbox

Logout of Dropbox to stop syncing your calorie entries.

  • Entries up to this point will remain on your Dropbox until you delete them manually.
  • You can decide to log in again to your Dropbox at a later time and all entries will be in sync again.
